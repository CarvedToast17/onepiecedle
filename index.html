How to Play

Guess the secret One Piece character. Each guess reveals clues:

✓Exact match
~Partial match (Haki) or close range (Bounty)
✗No match

Arrow hints (↑↓) show if Bounty or First Arc is higher or lower than the answer.

Affiliation — crew or faction
Devil Fruit — type or None
Haki — 👀 Observation · 💪 Armament · 👑 Conqueror's
Origin — sea of birth
Bounty — current bounty (₿)
First Arc — debut story arc
Gender — character gender

💡 Press N after solving to start a new game.
