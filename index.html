How to Play

Guess the secret One Piece character. Each guess reveals clue matches across the board.

One Piece Guess Who & Imposter Game

OnePieceWho is a free online One Piece game where you guess a secret character using clues like their bounty, devil fruit, origin, and crew. Play solo with unlimited guesses or a daily challenge, or go head-to-head with friends using the 1v1 Duel or Impostor multiplayer modes.

One Piece Impostor Mode

The One Piece imposter game mode is a pass-and-play party game for 3–4 players. A secret character is chosen — everyone knows who it is except one player: the impostor. The impostor receives only a single hint word to work with. Players take turns discussing the character, and the group must figure out who doesn’t actually know the answer before the impostor blends in.
